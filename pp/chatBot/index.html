Are you using the RSI criteria ?

Great, Please remember that when using the RSI criteria on the EA, you will not see trades open up straight away. Trades will only place when the RSI criteria is met.

To see the RSI please open up a second chart, change the timeframe of the chart to what ever the RSI is using on your settings, add the RSI indicator and configure the settings to suit what your using on your settings.

You will now be able to see when the EA should start a sequence based on when the RSI levels are hit.

Using a third party programme, all trades within the EDGE Strategy can be automated. So, every time a Strategy Alert pings, it will automatically open the trade for you, without you having to enter it yourself.

Mt4 or 5 is fine.
https://tradingview.to

They have install videos and guides on their website, so please give them a watch.

One licence per account and the Strategy Alert text you'll need to use is shown below. (Also Hedging off - to ensure a trade will close on a reverse signal).

{{strategy.market_position}} {{ticker}} q=0.3% sl={{plot_3}} tp={{plot_6}}

Q=percentage risk

Add a + after ticker if your symbols are GBPUSD+ for example - or whatever may follow them depending on your broker

On the EA itself remove the 500 pipettes SL and TP for the printed SL and TP to work - and ensure all pairs you're trading are in your 'Market Watch' symbol area.

Automating the strategy can really help when you're busy or sleeping!

It also helps with your trading psychology to stay disciplined.
