Faq

Think of us as the modern hedge fund.

We manage your money. You never pay us a percentage of profits and your money is never locked up. We are a dedicated team of asset managers in your back pocket. You give us your hard-earned capital. We invest it into wonderful businesses. The goal is to deliver high returns on this capital for you over the long run.

Create an account and transfer capital.

This takes roughly 24 hours (coming soon mobile app with lightning-fast onboarding speed and deposit capability 2 min). You can choose either a taxable account or a retirement account. Then link a bank account or send a wire transfer. This money goes into your own separately managed account. Some clients give us thousands, others give us hundreds of thousands. Your call.
