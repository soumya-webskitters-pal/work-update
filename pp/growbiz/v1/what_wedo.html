Email Marketing

Klaviyo / Manychat

What possibilities would open up if we told you there was a secret piece of real estate where you could talk to your customers without anyone else competing for their attention? What about if we told you it had been under your nose all along, responsible for roughly 20% of your revenue? Email marketing is like the wild west - the one place where there’s no algorithm, no rules, and no distractions.

With the right dedication, it’s an opportunity to create an intentional relationship with your customers. One where you show up to offer value time and time again and they find that they begin to long for that little buzz that tells them you’ve sent an email.

This puts the agency back in their hands and lets customers come to you on their own terms: when you’ve spent time investing in their trust and shown your value long before they hand over a single dollar. It’s amazing the kind of long term partnerships you can build with customers when you switch your mindset from looking for a quick sale to figuring out how you can serve them. We’d love to help you master it.
