about us

Thanks for visiting our website.

Let me tell you a little bit about me, the founder of GrowBiz.

A small country town in Japan

My name is Daisuke.
I was born in a small country town in Japan called Iwate where my parents and grandmother taught me early on the values of respect, integrity, and honesty — values that I use to inform every aspect of my life.

But I’m getting ahead of myself.
When I was 15 years old, I moved to Sunshine Coast, Australia where I finished school in Woombye, got my first job, and launched my first business (if you must know, it was a speed-dating event business).

Unfortunately, that business ended in miserable failure.
Why, you ask?

I Failed and
lost everything…

Because I didn’t market it.

Marketing is crucial to the success of any business regardless of how great the service or product is. Had I known that sooner then maybe my first business wouldn’t have ended the way it did. But no matter. I learned my lesson. I decided to sharpen my marketing skills by reading tons of books and attending dozens of seminars. Pretty soon I was marketing for local businesses and white-labelling for other marketing agencies.

It was great
At first…

Sick to my Stomach

You see, I really enjoyed the work but at the same, it made me feel sick to my stomach. This is because I saw firsthand how many agencies didn’t really have their client’s best interests at heart. What only matters to them is how they can benefit from the client, not how their client can benefit from them. They didn’t care about the results — all they cared about was taking in as many clients as possible without considering whether they can help them or not. They’d prey on their client’s vulnerabilities because they know how much the client needs a digital marketing agency. They’d overpromise and underdeliver. They’d ask the client to pay big dollars but then they’d outsource the work to cheap, low-quality contractors without telling the client about it. There was no transparency or integrity. At all. This made me really angry because I believe that nobody should ever get burnt by a marketing agency. Then it hit me: I had already worked on hundreds of campaigns and I knew the ins and outs of a typical marketing agency.

Why don’t I take the marketing
system into my own hands?

$17,000 turned into $95,000

So I built GrowBiz Marketing Agency in the hopes of changing people’s lives through marketing. With GrowBiz, we’ve successfully turned multiple e-commerce companies into 7-figure businesses using our methodology. Not only that — we’ve also taken brands from being barely known to being pretty famous. One of our recent success stories is YourIncomeSpace.com, an e-commerce brand that sells online programs. In just under TWO MONTHS we were able to scale their business from $25,000 to $95,000. The amount that we spent on advertising?

$17,000.
That’s an ROI Of 550%
