About Charlottetown

Charlottetown is the biggest city on Prince Edward Island and is the government and economic center of the province.

There are several key business areas in Charlottetown, which includes biotechnology and it is one of the fastest-growing sectors in the city. On top of the private investments in the city, the biotechnology cluster consists of the University of Prince Edward Island and the National Research Council. Charlottetown is also known for its tourism; a strong and diversified manufacturing industry, a rising financial service sector and the biggest service export industry on the Island. Therefore, Charlottetown has a lively nightlife, exceptional restaurants and a unique and diverse retail sector. It is an amazing coastal city for hosting meetings and conventions.
